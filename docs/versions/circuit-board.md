# Version: Circuit Board

## Primary Shape: Rectangle with Traces
A sharp rectangle that integrates connection points and "traces" (lines) extending from the edges.

## Geometry Specs
- **Corner Style**: Sharp (90 degrees).
- **Decorations**: External lines ending in dots (vias) at random intervals on the perimeter.
- **Background**: subtle circuit pattern opacity.

## Design Philosophy
Hardware-focused. Looks like a physical component soldered onto a motherboard.

## Animation
- **Hover**: Light pulse travels along the circuit traces.
