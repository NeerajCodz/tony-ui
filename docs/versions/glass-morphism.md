# Version: Glass Morphism

## Primary Shape: Frosted Rounded Rectangle
A standard rounded rectangle defined by its texture rather than its outline.

## Geometry Specs
- **Corner Style**: Rounded.
- **Radius**: `12px` (Soft).
- **Surface**: High blur backdrop (`backdrop-filter: blur(10px)`).
- **Border**: Semi-transparent white/gray (1px).

## Design Philosophy
Modern and clean. Gives a sense of depth and layering.
